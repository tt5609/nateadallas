Topic: Posture, Body Mechanics, and Back Pain
By: Elaine Tsay, SPT


Abstract: Using good posture and proper body mechanics to help reduce back pain in adults.
Elain Tsay is currently pursing her Doctor of Physical Therapy at Texas State University in San Marcos. She is in her 3rd semester of school and has more than 2 years of experience working in a physical therapy clinic. She will share her expertise and give some helpful tips on how to reduce back pain.
