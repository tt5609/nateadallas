======================================================================= Announcement

OBJECTIVE

To promote and encourage Taiwanese American/Taiwanese Students to pursue their professional career in areas of engineering, sciences and their related technologies

ELIGIBILITY

1.    Taiwanese Americans from Texas who are pursuing degree in American universities or students who are pursuing degree at the universities in Texas.

2.    One or both of the applicant’s parents, grandparents, or guardian must be born in Taiwan.  

3.    Applicant’s major must be in areas of engineering, physical/life sciences, medical science, mathematical science, computer and related technologies.

NUMBER OF SCHOLARSHIP AWARDS AND AMOUNT

1.    Up to three awards ($1,000 per award)

2.    Several honorable mentions ($100 per award)

 
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

1.   Completed Application Form

2.    Resume 

3.    Transcripts from the current or past attending university.

4.    Two recommendation letters including one from a member of any Taiwanese organizations in US or Taiwan.

DEADLINES

Application deadline: October 3rd,  2014

Award will be in NATEA annual meeting in November  2014

         Visit the web page at  http://natea.taadfw.org
