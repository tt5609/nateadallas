Tony Kuo’s bio:

Tony Kuo joined ReachLocal as a Marketing Expert in 2014 bringing over 15 years of a versatile background in both sales and account management experience. He has worked as Director of Business Development for a Dallas law firm where he developed his strong communication style working with various levels of leadership from senior to C-Level management. His experience working from privately held companies to Fortune 500 businesses equips Tony to develop innovative marketing strategies for companies across all industries. Tony exemplifies solid professional standards with an excellent track record of dependability and effectiveness in handling extreme pressure while delivering uncompromising quality of service to his clients. He has the distinctive ability to consistently maximize and achieve positive results while thriving in a challenging yet fast-paced environment. With his skills, experience, and expertise, he is confident that he will deliver critical strategies and significant contribution to all of his clients where financial success can be achieved.
